Company Information

At Totoys, we've developed a clever interactive solution with parental controls, content filters, and analytics to help you keep an eye on your kids' screen usage. We are using Artificial Intelligence and the Internet of Things technology to create an exciting new electronic toy that can read children's emotions and respond accordingly. Our app offers a variety of games, music, animation, videos, lessons, and more, all of which may be restricted by an adult's account. Tracking their children's media consumption daily might help parents identify their children's potential areas of interest far earlier. The solution seeks to foster the growth of the child's Intelligence Quotient, emotional intelligence, spiritual intelligence, resilience to adversity, and imaginative capacity.

We are developing a figurine Toy which is compatible with Alexa and Google Mini devices. The built-in sensors will allow us to track a child's sleep patterns, mood, amount of time spent being active, analysis of their interests, and so on. The cloud database will get these records. They'll undergo additional examination based on our character algorithms. Songs, stories, games, and lessons will be among the outcomes that keep their interest for more extended and contribute to positive character development. These contents can be tailored to their specific family. Toys with built-in cybersecurity defences will be one way we stand apart.
