5 everyday things we see AI in, which makes our life easier

The one term that has been baffling all parents since its inception – Artificial Intelligence! Contrary to the name, AI has become blended so well and is considered so natural in today’s tech-savvy world! It has made our lives comfortable in a way that we couldn’t have imagined! Social media has made the best use of it –processing lots of data to learn users’ preferences, target advertising, fighting cyberbullying, and enhancing the users’ experience in every way possible when they are online, AI hasn’t left any stone unturned in the last few years it became quite popular.

How could we forget the big help Digital Assistants (DA) have been donning the role of Personal Assistant – Apple’s Siri, Google Now, Amazon’s Alexa, and Microsoft’s Cortana…! You don’t have to miss your schedules, or important tasks anymore. These DAs’ take care of those for you from your every single interaction.

Elon Musk tried capturing the attention of passionate automobile enthusiasts with his Autopilot projects at Tesla. Can we even believe that approx. 6 billion transistors constitute the circuit of each Tesla chip and these autopilots enable cars to navigate through freeways and even traffic? Though not every project is a success, technology is exponentially bursting with new ideas and ways of making our life easier!

More than AI impacting lives all around us, the way it has found itself so attached to a child’s life cannot be underrated at all!! In this complex technical environment where parents are literally struggling to cope with the new technology being introduced in the market and the difficulty, they face in refusing to buy AI-based electronics for kids, Totoys proudly and happily brings to them an assistant that can listen and engage a child and parent as well in a responsible way!
